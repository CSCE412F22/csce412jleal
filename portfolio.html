Motorized Longboard

This is my motorized longboard project. It uses two 5s lithium polymer batteries, a 180 kv motor, a vesc speed controller, and two Arduino nanos communicating via nrf24l01 radio chips. It has a theoretical top speed of 20 mph, and a 10 mile range. I chose to use arduinos to control the vesc so that I would be able to create my own throttle handling functions and expand the functionality of the remote to do more than control the speed.

Hybrid Fuel Rocket

This is a project I worked on in highschool. I was a part of a 6 person team that begin designing a hybrid fuel rocket in August 2017. I was responsible for the electronics, nosecone design and fabrication, as well as the recovery deployment charges. We launched our rocket at White Sands Missle base in June 2018

Portable Aquaponics Unit

This is another project I was a part of. It is a portable aquaponics unit. aquaponics is an alternative niche form of agriculture that uses up to 90% less water than traditional agriculture. The unit was made portable so it could be used for education outreach by taking it to schools or events.
